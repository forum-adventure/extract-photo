GIF Export Settings

* Higher FPS means smoother animation but significantly increases file size. "Original" option maintains the source video smoothness.
* High resolution delivers crisp images and minimizes color pixelation. Exporting in "Original" quality can result in extremely large files if the video is Full HD/4K.

Processing Note: WebAssembly technology extracts GIFs entirely within your browser RAM. If you select High configurations or long video segments (>10s), the process may take from several seconds up to a minute. Please do not close the app!
